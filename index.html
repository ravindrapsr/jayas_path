About Us

Trusted Diagnostics in Karahari

Jayas Pathology is a trusted diagnostic centre located in the heart of Karahari village, Uttar Pradesh. We are committed to delivering fast, accurate, and affordable test results to the local community.

Under the guidance of Rajesh Kumar (Ranu Thakur), our lab maintains the highest standards of quality and hygiene. Our team is known for being approachable, helpful, and professional.

From basic blood tests to comprehensive health panels, we offer a full spectrum of pathological services — with the convenience of home sample collection and NFC digital payments.
